Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

BhaloGo ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our hyperlocal delivery service in Agartala, Tripura, including our website, WhatsApp ordering, and phone-based ordering (collectively, the "Service").

By using our Service, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Name: To identify you and personalize your experience.
  • Phone Number: For order communication, delivery coordination, and customer support via WhatsApp and calls.
  • Delivery Address: To accurately deliver your orders.
  • Order Details: Items ordered, prescriptions shared, and order history for service fulfillment.
  • Payment Information: UPI transaction IDs for payment verification. We do not store your UPI PIN, bank account details, or card information.

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution when accessing our website.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the website, and interaction patterns.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to remember your language preference and theme settings. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: Processing, confirming, and delivering your orders.
  • Communication: Sending order confirmations, delivery updates, and responding to your queries via WhatsApp or phone.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve our website, delivery operations, and customer experience.
  • Customer Support: Resolving complaints, processing refunds, and handling returns.
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements, including maintaining records as required under applicable Indian laws.
  • Safety & Security: Detecting and preventing fraudulent or unauthorized activity.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Delivery Partners: Your name, phone number, and delivery address are shared with our delivery agents solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order.
  • Partner Stores & Pharmacies: Order details (items, prescriptions) are shared with the relevant store to prepare your order.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of BhaloGo, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
  • Order communication via WhatsApp is protected by WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption.
  • Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

  • We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
  • Order Records: Maintained for a minimum of 12 months for customer support and legal compliance.
  • Prescription Records: Maintained as required under applicable pharmaceutical regulations.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

6. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of receiving promotional messages at any time by messaging "STOP" on WhatsApp.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided in Section 10.

8. Children's Privacy
